What Makes Powerhouse Different

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Powerhouse was born from the need for flexible, inspiring workspaces that foster collaboration and innovation. We've grown from a single location to a network of vibrant coworking hubs across the city.

Our Mission

To empower entrepreneurs, freelancers, and remote workers with more than just a desk - we provide the tools, community, and environment to help your business thrive.

Our Values

  • Collaboration over competition
  • Flexibility to grow at your pace
  • Community that supports and inspires
